About

PUBLISHED ON SEP 5, 2020

Wulan Aquariyanti was born and raised in Indonesia. She completed all her education up to her bachelor’s degree in Indonesia and began her professional journey with Nokia Siemens Networks.

She is passionate about teaching. During her undergraduate time she taught her fellow and junior students as a lecturer/lab assistant in Universitas Bina Nusantara University, her almamater. She was also active in social education for marginalized community in rural Jakarta.

Her activity in social activity and teaching still follow her when she moved to Singapore in 2009. She voluteered in AIDHA, organisation that empower foreign domestic employees and joined Indonesian Training Center under the Embassy of Indonesia, as an ESL tutor for Indonesian domestic employees. During her breastfeeding journey with her first child, she also volunteered as call center counsellor in BMSG, Singapore.

She is currently the lead ESL Tutor in Indonesian Training Centre and initiating a personal project to spread digital literacy to Indonesian foreign domestic employees in Singapore.
